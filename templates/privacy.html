Privacy Policy

Last updated:

MediDown.com is a universal media downloader interface. We aim to minimize data collection and use only what is necessary to provide the service.

Information We Collect

  • URLs you submit for analysis and download processing.
  • Basic technical logs (timestamps, IP address, user-agent) for abuse prevention and troubleshooting.

Cookies

We may use functional cookies for session and rate-limiting. If advertising is enabled, third-party cookies may be set by Google AdSense after you grant consent. You can manage consent using the banner on the site.

Analytics and Ads

If enabled by the site owner, we may use Google Analytics and Google AdSense. Analytics and personalized ads are disabled by default until you provide consent.

Third-Party Processors

  • Google Analytics — traffic measurement. Policies: Privacy, Cookies.
  • Google AdSense — advertising. Policies: Ads, Personalization.
  • Cloudflare (if used by the operator) — security and performance. Policies: Privacy.

Data Retention

Technical logs are retained for a limited period necessary for security and performance monitoring. Downloaded files and temporary processing artifacts are cleaned regularly.

Contact

For questions about this policy, contact the site operator via the contact method listed on the homepage or repository.
